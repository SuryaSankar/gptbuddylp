How to Use GPTBuddy

  • Open ChatGPT or any supported platform.
  • Click on the GPTBuddy sidebar toggle button to activate the extension.
  • Browse through your past questions and organize them easily.
  • Hover over the question to see detailed information.
  • Click on any question to quickly scroll to it in the conversation.
